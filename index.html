Quasiperiodic Functions & Torus Geodesics

Interactive Visualizations of Geodesic Curves on the Flat and Embedded Torus

Flat Torus Geodesics (2D)

Click anywhere to set the geodesic direction from the origin (bottom-left). The visualization features an interactive angle guide showing the angle and slope in real-time before you click. Watch how geodesics wrap on the flat torus, revealing periodic behavior for rational slopes and dense trajectories for irrational slopes.

Embedded Torus Geodesics (3D)

Click anywhere on the torus to set the geodesic direction from the origin (orange marker). The visualization shows how geodesics behave on the standard torus embedded in three-dimensional space, with synchronized curve and marker animation for both rational and irrational winding numbers.
